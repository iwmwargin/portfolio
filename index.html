About Me

My name is Eric Wargin and I am currently a student with the UNC Coding Boot Camp program. I've spent over twenty years working creatively in the kitchen, managing food, people, purveyors, customers and everything else related to the food world. I'm excited to pivot from the culinary world to the coding world. Years in the culinary business have taught me how to think on my feet, remain cool under pressure, extreme time managment and how to engage with coworkers, staff and the world at large. My multiple decades in food service have build a strong foundation that I will use to become a junior developer.
