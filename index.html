About Me

I am currently a student with the UNC - Chapel Hill Coding Boot Camp program. I've spent over twenty years working creatively in the culinary world, managing food, people, purveyors, customers and everything else related to the restaurant business. I have an A.S. in Culinary Arts from Johnson and Wales and am excited to transition into the coding world.

I am excited to bring my years of culinary creativity, people management, problem solving skills and crisis management to a new career. While cooking and coding are very different skill sets, I'm confident I can take what I've learned and apply it to code.
